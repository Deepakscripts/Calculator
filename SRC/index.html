Scientific Calculator

How To Use

Addition -:
  • For example, to enter the number 2, press the 2 key.
  • Press the addition operator (+) key.
  • To enter the number 3, press the 3 key.
  • Press the equals (=) key to display the result.
Substraction -:
  • For example, to enter the number 5, press the 5 key.
  • Press the substraction operator (-) key.
  • To enter the number 1, press the 1 key.
  • Press the equals (=) key to display the result.
Multliplication -:
  • For example, to enter the number 8, press the 8 key.
  • Press the multliplication operator (x) key.
  • To enter the number 8, press the 8 key.
  • Press the equals (=) key to display the result.
Division -:
